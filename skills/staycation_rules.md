# Forest Staycation Rules

## Content Goal
Forest staycation content must help readers decide:

- Is this place worth booking?
- Who should go?
- What hidden expectations exist?

Content priority:
Decision value > Real experience > Aesthetic

## Hook Structure

Hook must include:
- Nature setting (forest / river / cabin)
- Expectation gap OR hidden insight

Examples:
RM300森林小屋值不值？
KL人周末别只去商场
森林Cabin真实体验

Avoid:
Generic travel hooks like:
周末好去处
小众秘境
推荐打卡

## Core Script Structure

Point 1 – Environment Reality
- exact location
- distance from KL
- sound / temperature / vibe

Point 2 – Hidden Cost or Limitation
- price
- transport difficulty
- bugs / humidity / signal

Point 3 – Who It Is For

Examples:
✔ couples
✔ digital detox
✔ nature lovers

Not suitable for:
✘ luxury travellers
✘ strong aircon lovers
✘ people who hate insects

## Mandatory Element
Every staycation script must include:

- price anchor
- travel distance
- one downside

## Visual Guidance

Shots must show:
- cabin exterior
- forest environment
- real room interior
- morning atmosphere

Avoid:
pure aesthetic shots only
